Geswitch Telecomunications is an internet service provision company dedicated to serving internet consumers with the best experience all day everyday. The company is centered in Murera Village, serving hundreds of customers distributed across Matangi-ini, Ebenezer, Rurii, Kumura area, Junction, Biashara ACK and Prairie School area. Geswitch Telecomunications was founded by George Muhando in the year 2022, majoring in provision of links in form of wireless and fibre connections. Since then The company has been able to surpass competing ISP companies in the market and increasing their consumer count day after day. In the future, Geswicth is looking to take over the general networking market in Murera Village.
